Key Design Decisions

1

Minimal phase dots

Small dots show progress without counting steps. Green = done, blue = current, dark = ahead. Divider separates card phases from conversation.

2

Original text persists

THGNT/BHS auto-loads at step 2 and stays visible through steps 3-4 so Bryan can reference it while translating and digesting.

3

Seamless transition

After step 5, the card area smoothly becomes the conversation thread. No page reload, no modal. The AI opens with a contextual question based on Bryan's card responses.

4

Outline sidebar (collapsible)

Same outline sidebar from the existing study UI. Available in both card and conversation phases. Opens on right side when needed.
