A — Select Text → Popup

Highlight text, popup appears. Add a note, then either "Save" (keep for later) or "Discuss" (flag for conversation). Natural reading flow.

C — Side-by-side Notepad

Free-form notepad next to the study bible text. Most flexible — just type as you read. Less structured but matches how you might use a physical notebook.

Whichever you pick, saved notes/annotations get passed to the AI when conversation starts at phase 6 — so it knows what caught your eye and what you want to wrestle with.
